KSA Cricket academy was started on July 2016, with a view to impart and promote cricket education along with fitness and professional coaching in all the cities of Surendranagar District. The academy also works for promoting women cricket by giving free coaching and training them for better exposure in Women Cricket. The academy also provide coaching to specially able players such has partial handicap, deaf, dumb or partial blinds and enables them with good knowledge about cricket and gives them the opportunity to take it as a career option.
